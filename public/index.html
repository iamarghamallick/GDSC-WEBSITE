What Is Google DSC?

Helping students bridge the gap between Theory and Practice.

Google Developer Student Clubs are university based community groups for students interested in Google Dev technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a Google DSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.

How often does events take place?

Events are held very frequently by DSC BPPIMT. These events include various webinars, seminars, which caters every language and skill development domains. Google DSC bppimt also conducts inter-college coding competitions.

How to become a member of GDSC?

For becoming a member of this club, one should have a prominent skill and enthusiasm to work with everyone.You get a lot of opportunities after becoming a member of Google DSC. Each year our club open registration slots for New session and new members. Those who are interested can wait and join in the session 2024-2025.

What domains GDSC BPPIMT have?

Google DSC BPPIMT has a vast range of domains which includes Web development, App development, Tech (Python, Machine learning), Cloud ,Outreach, Management and Design.
